# Red Carpet Plumbing Brand Guide

## 1. Brand Overview

Briefly describe Red Carpet Plumbing as a Las Vegas plumbing company focused on reliable, responsive, customer-first plumbing services.

## 2. Brand Positioning

Explain the brand position:

Red Carpet Plumbing is a local Las Vegas plumbing company offering 24/7 plumbing services for homeowners and businesses throughout the Las Vegas Valley.

## 3. Primary Audience

Include:

- Homeowners
- Property managers
- Business owners
- Commercial property operators
- Real estate professionals
- Residents with urgent plumbing problems
- Customers searching for emergency plumbing help
- Customers comparing local plumbers

## 4. Brand Voice

Use these voice traits:

- Professional
- Helpful
- Trustworthy
- Local
- Clear
- Reassuring
- Responsive
- Practical
- Customer-first

## 5. Writing Style

Use:

- Clear sentence structure
- Short paragraphs
- Local service language
- Direct answers
- Plain-language explanations
- Strong CTAs
- SEO-friendly but natural wording
- Helpful FAQ-style answers

Avoid:

- Overly technical explanations without plain-language support
- Unverified claims
- Overpromising
- Fear-based language
- Keyword stuffing
- Em dashes
- Double hyphens
- Fake urgency
- Fake reviews or ratings

## 6. Brand Messaging Pillars

Include these pillars:

### 6.1 Fast Plumbing Help

Messaging should emphasize responsive plumbing support, especially for emergency plumbing needs.

### 6.2 Local Las Vegas Expertise

Messaging should connect the company to Las Vegas and the Las Vegas Valley.

### 6.3 Transparent Service

Messaging should support upfront communication, transparent pricing, and no hidden fees only where those claims are already stated or approved.

### 6.4 Customer Satisfaction

Messaging should reflect customer-first service, quality workmanship, and satisfaction-focused plumbing support.

### 6.5 Comprehensive Plumbing Solutions

Messaging should show that the company handles emergency, residential, commercial, repair, replacement, installation, and diagnostic plumbing services.

## 7. Approved CTAs

Include these CTA options:

- Call Now

- Get Instant Plumbing Help
- Request a Free Quote
- Schedule Service

- See How We Can Help

## 9. Service Area Language

Use approved service area language:

Red Carpet Plumbing serves Las Vegas, Henderson, North Las Vegas, Paradise, Summerlin, Spring Valley, Enterprise, Boulder City, Green Valley, Lake Las Vegas, and nearby communities throughout the Las Vegas Valley.

Also mention that older site content references Railroad Pass, Sunrise Manor, The Lakes, and Whitney, but these should be confirmed against the final approved service area strategy before being used heavily.

## 10. Local SEO and AEO Messaging Rules

Every page should clearly answer:

- What service is offered?
- Where is it offered?
- Who is it for?
- What problem does it solve?
- Why should the customer trust the company?
- What should the customer do next?

Use direct-answer sections for AEO and featured snippet support.

## 11. Trust Signal Rules

Allowed only if verified or already approved:

- Local, family-owned business
- Licensed plumbers
- Transparent pricing
- No hidden fees
- Customer satisfaction guarantee
- High-quality workmanship
- 24/7 emergency service
- Affordable pricing

Mark these as "verify before publishing" if supporting documentation is not available.

## 12. Schema and Claims Rules

- LocalBusiness and Plumber schema are allowed when NAP details are confirmed.
- Service schema is allowed when service pages contain matching visible content.
- FAQPage schema is allowed only when visible FAQs match exactly.
- HowTo schema is allowed only when visible step-by-step instructions exist.
- Review and AggregateRating schema are not allowed unless real, visible, verified reviews are present.
- Do not invent business facts.

## 13. Visual Brand Direction

Use the current brand identity as the foundation. If exact colors are not confirmed from logo files or design tokens, mark color values as pending confirmation.

Recommended visual direction:

- Professional
- Clean
- Trustworthy
- Local service business feel
- Strong CTA contrast
- Clear service cards
- Image-supported service sections
- Two-column hero layout where appropriate
- Contact or quote form on the right side of hero/CTA sections where appropriate

## 14. Image Rules

- Use real or approved plumbing-related images.
- Do not use images with readable text unless approved.
- Avoid fake badges, fake awards, or fake license graphics.
- Service cards should include image placeholders.
- Images should be organized by scope before being moved into public/images.
- Do not commit Images/ until approved.

## 15. Do Not Use

Do not use these unless explicitly approved:

- "Best plumber in Las Vegas" as a factual claim
- Fake review counts
- Fake star ratings
- Fake years in business
- Fake license numbers
- Fake warranties
- Unverified awards
- Unverified guarantees
- Em dashes
- Double hyphens

## 16. Example Brand Copy Patterns

Include several reusable examples:

### Homepage Intro Pattern

Red Carpet Plumbing provides responsive plumbing services for homes and businesses throughout Las Vegas and the surrounding communities. From emergency plumbing calls to water heater repairs, drain cleaning, leak detection, and pipe replacement, our team helps customers get reliable plumbing support when they need it.

### Service Page Intro Pattern

Need [service] in [location]? Red Carpet Plumbing helps homeowners and businesses diagnose the issue, explain the next steps, and complete the repair or installation with clear communication and professional care.

### Location Page Intro Pattern

Red Carpet Plumbing serves [location] with plumbing repair, installation, emergency service, and diagnostic support for homes, businesses, and local properties.

### CTA Pattern

Need plumbing help in Las Vegas? Call Red Carpet Plumbing today or request service online.

## 17. Brand Compliance Checklist

Before publishing any page, confirm:

- No em dashes
- No double hyphens
- No invented claims
- No invented reviews
- No invented ratings
- No invented license numbers
- FAQ schema matches visible FAQ content
- Service schema matches visible service content
- Location name is used naturally
- CTA is clear
- Internal links are included
- Page supports SEO, GEO, AEO, local search, and conversion
